Sweet Roll Dough (2 lb Osipoto BM8205)

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients in order: liquids → butter/egg → dry → yeast on top.
  2. Select Dough cycle on your Osipoto BM8205.
  3. Shape, final rise 30-60 min, bake 175–190°C (350–375°F) 15–25 min.
